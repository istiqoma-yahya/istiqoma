# Istiqoma — إستقامة

> **"Steadfastness"** — Your daily companion for spiritual growth and Islamic self-improvement.

**Live app → [istiqoma.com](https://istiqoma.com)**

---

## What is Istiqoma?

Istiqoma (إستقامة) is a spiritual self-improvement app for Muslims. It brings together everything you need to build and maintain consistent Islamic habits — all in one dark-themed, mobile-friendly Progressive Web App.

---

## Features

### 📖 Full Qur'an Reader with Audio
Read the complete Qur'an with a built-in audio recitation player — anytime, anywhere, without needing a separate app.

### 🧠 Qur'an Memorization (Hifz)
Practice and strengthen your memorization with an interactive tool that lets you hide and reveal verses. Record your own voice to assess and review your recitation and tajweed.

### 🕌 Daily Good Deed Tracking
Log and track your daily acts of worship and good deeds. Build consistency through a streak system and earn points as you progress on your spiritual journey.

### 🎯 Personal Targets
Set meaningful commitments for your deeds. Define your own targets so you stay accountable and have a clear sense of purpose for each day.

### 📊 Progress Analytics
Visualize your spiritual growth over time through beautiful charts. See patterns in your deed history, track streaks, and celebrate your consistency.

### 📚 Islamic Quizzes
Test and expand your knowledge of Islam through engaging quizzes covering a wide range of topics — great for learning and reflection.

### 🕐 Prayer Time Reminders
Get accurate, location-based prayer time notifications so you never miss a salah.

### 📿 Dzikir Counter
Keep count of your dhikr with a simple, distraction-free digital tasbih — perfect for post-prayer remembrance and daily wird.

### 🧭 Qibla Finder
Instantly find the direction of the Qibla wherever you are using your device's compass.

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Progressive Web App (PWA), mobile-first, dark-themed UI
- **Backend:** Firebase / Supabase
- **Platform:** Runs entirely in the browser — installable on any device

---

## Getting Started

Visit **[istiqoma.com](https://istiqoma.com)** — no download required. Install it as a PWA from your browser for the full app experience.
